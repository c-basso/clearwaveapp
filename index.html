Frequently Asked Questions

Will this damage my speakers?

No, the sound waves are safe and won't damage your speakers. It's basically the same as playing music on your phone, but with carefully calibrated frequencies designed specifically for water ejection.

Does this work on all devices?

Clear Wave works on iPhone and iPad devices. The app is optimized for iOS and provides the best results on Apple hardware. It requires iOS 15.1 or later.

How long should I play the sound to remove water?

Usually 10-30 seconds is enough, but you can repeat the process if needed. You can test your speaker volume after each cycle to check if the sound has improved.

What if the water ejection doesn't work?

If water ejection doesn't work immediately, try repeating the process 2-3 times. For stubborn water, you can also try the frequency tone generator at different frequencies. If the problem persists, it might indicate hardware damage that requires professional repair.

Is the app free to use?

Clear Wave is free to download with basic water ejection features. Premium features like the advanced tone generator are available through in-app purchases for power users.

Can I use this for other audio testing?

Yes! Clear Wave includes professional audio tools for testing hearing, tuning audio gear, monitoring noise levels, and checking stereo channel balance - making it an all-in-one audio toolkit.
